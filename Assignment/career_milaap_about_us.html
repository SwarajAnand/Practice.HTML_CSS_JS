    • About Us

    CareerMilaap is an online placement preparation & skills training start-up for college students which helps them to prepare for most demanding private jobs that do not require any prior experience with respect to their education. We focus on job profiles such as Business Development Executive, Web Designer, Graphic Designer, Digital Marketing Executive, Search Engine Optimization Executive, Content writer and many more. Our founder, Aarunya Rastogi is a Google certified Digital Marketing Expert and having corporate experience with multinational company named as uCertify.

    Our Vision

    It has been so long watching most of the final year students of different academic streams would not be able to get jobs in their core fields, which leave them unemployed for long time. Now, as the idea of CareerMilaap has evolved, the students will get another opportunity to prepare for the jobs, which are easy to get and have a humongous scope in future by learning the combination of desired skills in two to three months. We also understand that it is not possible for students to take training courses of high worth, as they do not make money. Therefore, we have designed our fee structure, which is affordable to students.
