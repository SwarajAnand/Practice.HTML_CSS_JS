How Can you Pay?

Pre-enrollment payment policy:

You can pay full fee of the training during registration. If you are unable to pay full fee at one time. It can be paid in two installments. The half fee, during the time of registration and the remaining half before the training of the next month begins.

Post-placement payment policy:

You need to pay an amount of Rs 2500 as registration fee and you can start your training with us. As soon as you get placed after the completion of training, you will pay us 50% of your first month salary
